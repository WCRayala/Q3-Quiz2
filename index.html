Wonka (2023): Fantasy/Musical - 1hr 56mins
Timothée Chalamet - Willy Wonka

Calah Lane - Noodle

Paterson Joseph - Slugworth

Matt Lucas - Prodnose

Mathew Baynton - Fickelbruber

 Joleta Petty: The movie, starring Timothee Chalamet as Wonka, showcases his talent and showcases his range. Matthew Baynton and Simon Farnaby's hilarious songs, particularly "Sweet Tooth," add to the overall whimsy in a dreary world. The plot is simple enough for a child to follow, but chaotic enough for Wonka to be a character. The movie brings nostalgia and hope to the original Charlie And The Chocolate Factory.
Rating:
4.5★

Spider-man: Across the Spider-verse (2023): Action/Comedy - 2hrs 20mins
Shameik Moore - Miles Morales

Hailee Steifeld - Gwen Stacy

Oscar Isaac - Miguel O'hara

Jason Schartzman

 Matthias Zeng: The Spider-Verse movie is a breathtaking experience with great characters, stunning visuals, and captivating music. The story is simple yet complex, making it easy for viewers to understand. The characters are fleshed out, with Gwen feeling like the main character, and Miles' character is well-written and handled perfectly. The visuals are so detailed that they can feel overwhelming at times, but this is a positive aspect. The movie showcases the love and detail put into each universe and character, with each one animated in a unique way. The film is unironically the best movie I've seen in a long time and is definitely up there.
Rating:
5★

Ghostbusters: Afterlife (2021): Comedy/Fantasy - 2hrs 4mins
Mckenna Grace - Phoebe Spengler

Paul Rudd - Mr. Grooberson

Paterson Joseph - Slugworth

Matt Lucas - Prodnose

Mathew Baynton - Fickelbruber

 Tolkien Nerd: enjoyed the movie Ghostbusters, with Justin Reitman's performance and Harold Ramis' dedication being heartwarming. The film had nostalgic referencing without overdoing it, and Paul Rudd's cameo was not overdone. Visual effects balanced old-school 80s with modern CGI without overdoing it. Olivia Wylds' character was well-portrayed, and the casting choice was good. Overall, it's a 5/5 recommendation.
Rating:
4★
